Meet the Owner

My name is Eunajem, and hair has been a part of my life since I was eight years old. I began styling family and friends and honed my craft over the years in my mother’s and sister’s hair. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion and, ultimately, my profession.

I founded Eunajem Studios to offer young women a place where they can cultivate their talent, build confidence and become self‑sufficient. Impact means everything to me, and empowering others through beauty is my way of making a difference.

Today, Eunajem Studios is a welcoming salon in the heart of London that serves clients of all ages and backgrounds. We pride ourselves on creating beautiful styles, nurturing up‑and‑coming stylists and fostering a positive, inclusive environment.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to empower our clients and team members through quality hair care, education and community. We believe confidence begins with loving the way you look and feel. That’s why we:

  • Value inclusivity and serve adults, children and everyone in between.
  • Prioritise healthy hair and protective styles that celebrate natural beauty.
  • Invest in training and mentorship for our stylists to grow in their craft.
  • Create a safe, welcoming space where creativity and individuality flourish.
