Our Story

I’m Eunajem, the founder of Eunajem Studios. From the age of eight, I’ve been perfecting the art of hair, starting with family and friends and eventually turning my passion into a thriving salon. Eunajem Studios exists to empower young women and cultivate emerging talent, while providing an inclusive space for clients of all ages. We believe beautiful hair builds confidence and independence.
